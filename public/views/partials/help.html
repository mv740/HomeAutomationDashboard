Help guide


  • Set service setting

    Before being able to add service tile to your dashboard, you must first initialise them by configuring their settings.

  • Integrate your services with your dashboard

    After having configured a service, you can go add it to your dashboard. Go to the services page and use the add a service form to add your specific service tile.
    You can only add a type of service that was already configured ( see step 1)

  • Modify your account information

    Want to change your password or username, then go to your Account Setting page.
